About Us

2010

Where it all began!

Fruit Fiesta planted its roots in a small kitchen with a vision to revolutionize the juice industry. The founders, inspired by a love for fresh, natural ingredients, laid the foundation for what would become a flavorful journey.

2012

A Splash into the Juicy World

The debut of our flagship product, Orange Burst, marked the first splash in the market. The vibrant blend quickly gained popularity, setting the standard for the quality and taste that Fruit Fiesta would become known for.

2014

Expanding the Fruit Fiesta Spectrum

Responding to the growing demand for variety, Fruit Fiesta expanded its product line. From the tang of Berry Bliss to the exotic twist of Tropical Fusion, our juices became a palette of flavors, catering to diverse tastes.

2016

Eco-Friendly Packaging Unveiled

Embracing responsibility, Fruit Fiesta introduced eco-friendly packaging, committing to reduce our environmental impact. Sustainability became an integral part of our ethos.

2018

Fruit Fiesta Goes Digital

Recognizing the digital era, Fruit Fiesta launched its online store, making our products accessible to juice enthusiasts nationwide. Convenience met quality as customers could now order their favorite blends with just a click.

2020

Nurturing Nature's Goodness

A milestone moment as Fruit Fiesta embraced organic farming practices. This shift ensured that every bottle encapsulated the pure essence of nature, free from pesticides and chemicals.

2022

Personalized Subscriptions Unleashed

A decade of Fruit Fiesta celebrated with the introduction of personalized juice subscriptions. Our loyal customers could now enjoy their favorite blends regularly, tailored to their preferences.
